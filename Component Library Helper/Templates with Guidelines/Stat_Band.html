Use When

  • Enumerating a few key features or attributes of a product or service. 
  • Adding visual weight to top-of-page messaging about a product or service. 

Don't Use When

  • Listing all the features or attributes of a product or service. Use a Data Table or Product Comparison Table.

Choose small, large, or mixed stat blocks. It is okay to mix and match block types to fit your content. 

Mixed stat block (default)

Number: Plain text.

  • Character limit is 10.
  • Examples:
    • 500PB
    • 90%
    • 0

Metric: Plain text.

  • Character limit is 45. Keep it as short as possible.
  • No capitalization. No period. 
  • Examples:
    • reduction in data
    • unplanned downtime
    • less hardware and electricity cost
Large stat block

Rich text

  • Character limit is 60. Keep it as short as possible.
  • One line only. No paragraphs. 
  • Sentence case. No period.
  • Examples:
    • Increased cloud storage from 2PB to 14PB
    • Faster customer deployments
Small stat block

Rich text

  • Character limit is 60. Keep it as short as possible.
  • Can have multiple lines (paragraphs) within the character limit. 
  • Sentence case. No period.
  • Examples:
    • Transition of SAP to Azure in three months and 60% reduction of database size
    • Increased cloud storage from 2PB to 14PB
    • Faster customer deployments
