Use When

  • Communicating the value proposition of a product or solution at the top of a page.

Don’t Use When

  • Communicating secondary messages or calls to action. Consider using Callout. 
  • Introducing sections instead of the entire page. Consider using Section Header. 
  • In the hero area. Use appropriate components, such as Full Hero or Title.
