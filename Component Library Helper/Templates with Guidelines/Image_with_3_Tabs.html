Alt text:  

 

 

 

 

Use When

  • Making three different points about a product at the same level of detail. The points need not be directly related.
  • Highlighting ways to connect with NetApp, such as Blogs, Events, and Community.  

Don’t Use When

  • Displaying 2 panes of related information. Use Side x Side 2 Tabs instead.
  • Displaying multiple panes of related information or telling a sequential story. Use Tabbed Band instead.
  • Multiple images are needed to reinforce the content. Use Tabbed Band instead.
