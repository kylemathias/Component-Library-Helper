A navigation component that presents a diverse list of destinations under a headline.

 

 

Template Copy Block

 

 

Component name: Offset cards                                 

Guidelines:

Headline (required)- Character limit is 30

Tiles

  • Minimum 2 tiles. No maximum.
  • Tile Headline (required)- up to 30 characters. Must be key-term relevant. 
  • Tile Body (optional)- up to 60 characters. Rich text field. Superscript and bold okay. No other formatting, linking, or bullets allowed. Links will break this component.
  • Tile CTA (required)- up to 15 characters. Text field. Label required.

Use When

  • Presenting an array of links at the conclusion of a page.

Do not Use When

  • Presenting links as the sole content of a page. Use Feature Tiles instead.
  • Presenting links within the main content of a page. Use Image with Tiles or Card Band instead.
