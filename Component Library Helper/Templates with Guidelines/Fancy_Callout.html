Use When

  • Repeating the page's primary call to action near the conclusion of the page.
  • Offering alternatives to the primary call to action, such as by composing Fancy Callout above a Card Band near the conclusion of the page. 

Don’t Use When

  • Offering a primary call to action for the first time near the top of the page. Instead, use the Hero Full. 
  • Promoting secondary or tertiary actions mid-page. Instead, use Callout (has no image) or Side x Side (has image).
